Data Scientist

Job Description

  • Analyze complex datasets to extract insights and support data-driven decision-making.
  • Develop predictive models and machine learning algorithms to solve business problems.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define objectives, gather data, and deliver actionable insights.

Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
  • Proficiency in programming languages (e.g., Python, R) and experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).
  • Strong knowledge of machine learning, statistical analysis, and data wrangling techniques.

Lastest Research

14 September 2024

Impact of Diet

Recent studies have identified a link between the consumption of ultra-processed foods and an increased risk of type 2 diabetes. Specifically, savory snacks, processed meats, ready-to-eat meals, and sweetened beverages have been associated with a higher incidence of the condition.

8 January 2025

Impact of Sugary Drinks on Type 2 Diabetes Incidence

In Nature Medicine reveals that one in ten new cases of type 2 diabetes globally in 2020 can be attributed to the consumption of sugary beverages, including soft drinks, fruit drinks, energy drinks, and lemonades. The research underscores the need for public health interventions to reduce the intake of these beverages to mitigate diabetes risk.

9 January 2025

Advancements in Treatment

Richard DiMarchi's work has led to significant progress in diabetes treatment, including the development of fast-acting insulins like Humalog and medications such as Ozempic and Mounjaro that manage type 2 diabetes without insulin and aid in weight loss.
